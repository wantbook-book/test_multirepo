Everything can be placed on the side of:

- Beacon
- Glass
- Tinted Glass
- Glowstone
- Ice
- Sea Lantern
- Doors
- Trapdoors(top or bottom, open)
- Barrier
- Composter
- Observer

Everything cannot be placed on the side of:

- Azalea
- Scaffolding
- End Rod
- Snow Layer (2-7)
- Heads
- Chorus Plant
