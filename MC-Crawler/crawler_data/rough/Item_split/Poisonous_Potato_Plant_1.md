### 
If two poisonous potato plants are placed in a container, like a chest, then the next time player opens that container, a renamed piece of paper appears in it. Each next time paper has a different message:

- Are you a plant?
- Do you think <PLAYERNAME> knows?
- I think they know
- Let's stay quiet
- Destroy this message after reading

## Notes
1. ↑Despite this, the paper does not disappear from player's inventory.
2. ↑"Right Button" uses the translation textkey.use, so it will change based on the selected keybind for "Use Item/Place Blocks" and the user's language.
