Renewability refers to the possibility of an item or block to be obtained continuously without exploring new areas or having additional players join. There exist 2 pages with an explanation of it:

- Renewable resource: Resources that can be renewed onSurvivalandAdventure, without the use of cheats or duplication exploits.
- Non-renewable resource: Resources that can't be renewed on those modes.

The Renewability of an item or block includes the use of farms (including Automatic farms) and factors in elements such as technically limited but abundant resources.
